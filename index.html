Problem

People with amnesia often struggle to recognize familiar faces, which can lead to difficulties in identifying friends, family, or regular visitors at their home. This inability can severely disrupt their social interactions and compromise their safety.

Solution

By integrating facial recognition technology into a door camera system, our solution aims to help individuals with amnesia easily identify visitors, enhancing their independence, security, and confidence in managing daily social interactions at their doorstep.
